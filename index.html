Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Nairobi and its surrounding metropolitan areas. For larger projects, such as industrial or agricultural installations, we can discuss service availability in other regions. Please contact us to confirm if we can service your specific location.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, absolutely. Barriam Electricals is a fully licensed and insured electrical contractor. This ensures that all our work complies with local regulations and safety standards, providing you with peace of mind and protection.

Do you provide a warranty for your work?

Yes, we stand by the quality of our workmanship. All our installations and services come with a comprehensive workmanship warranty. The specific duration may vary depending on the type of service, but we guarantee our work against defects for a specified period. Parts and equipment are covered by the manufacturer's warranty.

How much does a typical installation cost?

The cost varies significantly based on the project's scope, complexity, and the materials required. For example, a simple house rewire will cost less than a full solar panel installation. We provide free, no-obligation quotes after a thorough on-site assessment to give you an accurate and fair price.

What should I do in an electrical emergency?

If you are experiencing an electrical emergency (such as a burning smell from an outlet, sparks, or a power outage to a part of your home), first ensure your safety by staying away from the affected area. If possible, switch off the main power at your circuit breaker. Then, call us immediately on our 24/7 emergency line.

Do you offer maintenance services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance services for all electrical and solar systems. Regular maintenance is crucial for ensuring safety, efficiency, and longevity. We can schedule periodic inspections and servicing to keep your systems running optimally.
